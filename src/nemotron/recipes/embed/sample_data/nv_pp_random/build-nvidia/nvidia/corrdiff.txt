 Corrector Diffusion (CorrDiff) US GEFS-HRRR model down-scales several surface and atmospheric variables from 25-km resolutionforecast data from the Global Ensemble Forecast System (GEFS) and predicts 3-kmresolution High-Resolution Rapid Refresh (HRRR) data.  CorrDiff US allows the prediction of high-fidelity stochastic weather phenomena overthe CONUS from low-fidelity input data that would otherwise require expensive regionalnumerical simulations. CorrDiff is a generative downscaling model trained over the contiguous United States (CONUS). This model is ready for commercial use.
